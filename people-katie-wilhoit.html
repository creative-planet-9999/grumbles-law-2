Katie Wilhoit

PARTNER

E-mail: kwilhoit@adamsgrumbles.com

Katie is a commercial business attorney with extensive experience counseling small to mid-sized companies and entrepreneurs in matters involving contract drafting and risk-allocation, and indemnity and insurance matters, as well as privacy and data protection laws, protection of trade secrets, and complying with federal and state consumer protection laws. Katie has also developed 5+ years of experience in the specialized area of numismatic law and legal issues relating to investing and collecting precious metals, coins and currency.

Katie brings a common sense and practical approach to her legal practice, which she has developed over her years in various legal roles: from trial lawyer and partner in a large Minneapolis law firm, to claim attorney representing one of the U.S.'s largest property and casualty insurers, to general counsel for a Minneapolis-based direct-to-consumer marketing company.

Katie was born and raised in Minnesota and attended high school at the Convent of the Visitation high school in Mendota Heights, MN. Katie received her undergraduate degrees in History and French, cum laude, from DePauw University in Greencastle, IN; and her J.D., magna cum laude, from William Mitchell College of Law in St. Paul, MN.
