Paul Feng

PATENT AGENT

E-mail: pfeng@adamsgrumbles.com | Phone: 612-465-8090

Paul is a registered patent agent who has helped inventors protect their work in a wide range of technologies, including optical films, HVAC systems, RF coils for MRI, optical surveillance systems, MEMS & semiconductor sensors, RFID, energy systems, internal-combustion engines, micro-fluidic sample analyzers, sporting equipment, medical devices in various spaces, and software-implemented inventions in a range of fields such as geolocation, financial management and transactions, online training, mobile connectivity, and algorithm-driven control systems.

Paul enjoys meeting with inventors to hear their stories and learn the details that distinguish their work from what has come before, so that he can best tell the stories of their inventions to patent examiners and the world.
