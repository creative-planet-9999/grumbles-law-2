Our Core Practice Areas

Grumbles Law partners with clients and their co-council to develop innovative, forward thinking solutions in the areas of corporate, strategic IP, and finance.

Corporate

Starting/running a business can have a significant number of pitfalls. There are countless decisions to be made, and we can help. When it comes to policies and best practices Grumbles Law is the attorney service you want. We are constantly on call and can assure you that your business is safe in our hands.

  • Business formation, including entity selection and filing
  • Preparation of shareholder, partnership, limited liability company and other agreements
  • Negotiation and drafting of business agreements and contracts
  • Negotiation and drafting of agreements for asset and sales transactions, mergers, consolidations, reorganizations, liquidations, and similar restructurings

Strategic IP

You had the great idea, let Grumbles Law protect it. We help establish your company assets, file for trademarks, and protect your trade secrets. Coca-Cola may keep their flavor recipe in a vault, but you can assure that your secrets are just as safe with us.

  • Enterprise IP evaluation, including identification and ownership of IP assets
  • Patent prosecution and portfolio development
  • Trademark clearance and protection
  • Confidentiality and trade secret protection
  • IP licensing and transfer
  • IP enforcement/litigation

Finance

Businesses will need capital to sustain themselves. We help you not get bogged down by the details. We have helped numerous companies achieve successful rounds of funding, helped with regulatory issues and help with Minnesota Angel Tax Credit. We help you succeed every step of the way.

  • Planning and documentation for equity and debt offerings
  • Venture capital investment issues
  • Private equity issues
  • Minnesota Angel Tax Credit Certification
  • Regulatory issues
