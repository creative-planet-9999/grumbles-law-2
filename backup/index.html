Our Story

Grumbles Law aspires to be the leading innovative law firm by building long-term relationships with clients based on ethics, trust, and the highest standards of creativity and professionalism. Our customers are extremely passionate about the work they are doing, and we strive to match their enthusiasm and passion on every level. The clients that Grumbles Law works with are seen as thought leaders in their given industry, are driven by an internal mission and in all cases have a beautiful entrepreneurial spirit.

Why Clients
Choose Our Firm

Grumbles Law has a flexibility that is unmatched in the current market at meeting our client’s needs. We tailor every solution correspondingly to dedicate our best people to the task at hand. We have a blend of extraordinary people coupled with an in-depth knowledge of business, legal practices, corporate, strategic I.P. and Finance.

Experience

Grumbles Law offers our clients world class corporate, finance, and strategic intellectual property services. Our intellect, education, and experience are in line with the largest law firms in the nation. At Grumbles Law, we areentrepreneurs ourselves. We understand the passion and commitment it takes to build a business, and we show you that same passion and commitment to excellence in our practice. We wake up every morning excited to work with hungry entrepreneurs, and we can’t wait to work with you next!

Entrepreneurship
Focused

A business focused I.P. centric attorney can be a strategic partner to your business at any stage. It’s worthwhile to consult an attorney before making decisions that could have legal ramifications for your business. We will help you set up a business entity, obtain your trademarks or patents, review partnership agreements and much more! We are so passionate about seeing individuals take their first step to entrepreneurship, call us today!

Client Centered

If you have worked with an attorney in the past, you know that it can be an expensive undertaking. We position ourselves at a price point that is affordable for business owners of all sizes and stages. We aren’t just looking at our bottom line like most of the big firms. Grumbles law wants your business to succeed, and we enjoy watching your company grow and change. Grumbles law is an agile partner in that we will adapt to the ever-changing needs of your business.
